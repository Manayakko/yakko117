Ebb-and-Flow Protocols A Resolution of the Availability-Finality Dilemma

The availability-finality dilemma

The availability-finality dilemma refers to the challenge of designing a consensus protocol for a blockchain that is both live under dynamic participation and safe under temporary network partitions. In other words, it is difficult to achieve a protocol that can support a fully available ledger while also ensuring the finality of transactions.
可用性最终困境是指为区块链设计共识协议的挑战，该协议既可以在动态参与下运行，又可以在临时网络分区下安全。 换句话说，很难实现一个既能支持完全可用的账本，又能保证交易的最终性的协议。

Traditional consensus protocols, such as permissioned BFT protocols, prioritize safety under network partition but are not live under dynamic participation. On the other hand, dynamically available protocols, like Nakamoto’s Proof-of-Work protocol, prioritize liveness under dynamic participation but are not safe under network partition.
传统的共识协议，例如 许可型 BFT 协议，在网络分区下优先考虑安全性，但在动态参与下却无法生存。 另一方面，动态可用的协议，如中本聪的工作量证明协议，在动态参与下优先考虑活跃性，但在网络分区下并不安全。

To resolve this dilemma, the authors propose a new class of consensus protocols called ebb-and-flow protocols. These protocols aim to support a dynamically available ledger along with a finalized prefix ledger. The finalized ledger falls behind the available ledger during network partitions but catches up when the network heals.
为了解决这个困境，作者提出了一类新的共识协议，称为潮起潮落协议。 这些协议旨在支持动态可用的分类账以及最终确定的前缀分类账。 最终确定的账本在网络分区期间落后于可用账本，但在网络修复时赶上。

The authors also discuss Gasper, the candidate protocol for Ethereum 2.0’s beacon chain, and its design goals. Gasper combines the Casper FFG finality gadget with the LMD GHOST fork choice rule to achieve both finalization and availability. However, the authors identify an attack in the standard synchronous network model, highlighting a general difficulty with existing finality-gadget-based designs.
作者还讨论了以太坊 2.0 信标链的候选协议 Gasper 及其设计目标。 Gasper 将 Casper FFG 最终确定性小工具与 LMD GHOST 分叉选择规则相结合，以实现最终确定和可用性。 然而，作者在标准同步网络模型中发现了一种攻击，凸显了现有基于最终性小工具的设计的普遍困难。

In their construction of ebb-and-flow protocols, the authors propose using an off-the-shelf dynamically available protocol to maintain the available ledger and a separate off-the-shelf BFT protocol to finalize a prefix. They introduce the concept of snap-and-chat, where nodes take snapshots of the growing available ledger and input them into the BFT protocol for finalization. The authors show that this construction achieves optimal resilience and security guarantees.
在潮起潮落协议的构建中，作者建议使用现成的动态可用协议来维护可用的账本，并使用单独的现成的 BFT 协议来最终确定前缀。 他们引入了快照和聊天的概念，其中节点拍摄不断增长的可用账本的快照并将其输入 BFT 协议中以进行最终确定。 作者表明，这种结构实现了最佳的弹性和安全保证。

Overall, the ebb-and-flow protocols aim to resolve the availability-finality dilemma by providing a flexible consensus solution that supports both liveness and safety in a dynamic blockchain environment.
总体而言，潮起潮落协议旨在通过提供灵活的共识解决方案来解决可用性最终困境，该解决方案支持动态区块链环境中的活性和安全性。
